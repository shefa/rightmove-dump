Property description

This newly refurbished property offers a bright and attractive living room which has access to the balcony and leads through to a separate, modern, fitted, eat-in kitchen with stylish stainless steel work tops. There is a good size double bedroom with large, built-in wardrobes, a contemporary bathroom with a power shower and a wonderful walk-in storage room.

Calshot Street is superbly located for easy access to Kings Cross as well as being within walking distance to the Angel Station and the areas many amenities.

The property is available now and is offered furnished.
